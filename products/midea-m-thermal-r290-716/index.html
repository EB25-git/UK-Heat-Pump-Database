Notes

M Thermal R290 monobloc air-to-water heat pump for domestic and light commercial heating and hot water.

Output at A-2/W50 is 11.3 kW. SCOP/class at 35°C is 4.67; SCOP/class at 50°C is 3.62. Supply 220-240/1/50; recommended fuse 32 A. Hot water temperatures up to 75°C. Sales sheet notes M Thermal supports heating, domestic hot water, underfloor heating, built-in energy monitoring, Wi-Fi/Midea Smart Home app and quiet operation. EER/cooling performance not separately published in the supplied sheet.
