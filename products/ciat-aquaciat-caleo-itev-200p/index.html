Notes

High-temperature reversible air-to-water monobloc heat pump using natural R290 refrigerant for heating, cooling and domestic hot water applications.

Heating COP uses HA1 condition: outside air 7°C db/6°C wb, water 30/35°C, EN14511-3:2022. SCOP uses 30/35°C EN14825 average climate. EER uses CA1 cooling condition 12/7°C water at 35°C outdoor air. Unit weight is standard unit without variable-speed pump option.
