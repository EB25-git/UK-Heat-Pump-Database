Notes

Samsung EHS Mono HT Quiet R32 monobloc outdoor heat pump for high-temperature residential heating and cooling.

Cooling capacity 14.0 kW, EER 4.46, cooling power input 3.14 kW. Sound pressure 47 dBA; refrigerant charge 3.3 kg R32. Cooling operating range 10~46°C; heating and DHW -30~43°C. Heating LWT 15~70°C; cooling LWT 5~25°C. MCA/MFA 32.0/35.2 A; cooling/heating current 14.87/13.1 A; airflow 95 CMM. Samsung page states HT Quiet can provide leaving water temperature to 70°C.
