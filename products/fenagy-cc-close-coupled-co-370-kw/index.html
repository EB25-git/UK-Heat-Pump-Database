Notes

Close-coupled CO₂ air source heat pump with integrated V-shape upflow evaporators. 370 kW heating and 245 kW cooling. Compact single-unit design combining rack and evaporators for simplified installation.

R744 CO₂ GWP 1. 3 compressors, VSD optional. 4 evaporators with EC fans and cold gas defrost. Siemens PLC, Modbus/Profinet. 130 kg refrigerant charge. Design pressure 140/80 bar. Evaporator LpA approx 46 dB(A).
