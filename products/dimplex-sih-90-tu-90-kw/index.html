Notes

Dimplex SIH 90 TU high-efficiency ground-to-water (brine) heat pump, 90 kW, commercial.

Large commercial high-temperature brine-to-water heat pump (GDD platform, 3-phase). Two performance levels, flow to 70 C for heating and hot water. Cascadable for larger loads. Part of the SIH TU COM / SI TUR reversible range (e.g. SI 35/50/70/85 TUR, SI 130 TUR+). EN 14825 figures to be confirmed.
