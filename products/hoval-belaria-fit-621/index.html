Modulating compact air/water heat pump for outdoor installation, suitable for heating and cooling in cascades.

Output modulation 30-100%. A2/W35 heat output range 25.4-53.2 kW and COP 3.4; A-7/W35 heat output 40.5 kW, COP 2.8. SCOP 35/55°C 4.41/3.19; SEER cooling 4.3.
